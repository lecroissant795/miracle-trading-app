---

### Safe Wording Guidelines (MANDATORY)
Agents must use conditional language:
- “Where available”
- “Availability varies by region”
- “Depends on venue rules”
- “Eligible instruments and venues”
- Avoid claiming:
  - Guaranteed real-time data
  - Zero latency
  - Universal market coverage
  - Specific performance metrics unless measured

## 11. FAQ Section

### Q1: How do I get started with Miracle?
Create an account, complete verification, fund your wallet, and explore supported markets. You’ll see estimated fees before placing a trade.

### Q2: What fees will I pay?
We show an estimated breakdown before you submit an order (e.g., execution fees, FX conversion, and applicable third-party fees). Full details are available on the pricing page.

### Q3: Is the market data real-time?
Data availability depends on venue rules. We provide fast quote refresh and clearly label whether a market is real-time or delayed where applicable.

### Q4: Do you offer 24/5 trading?
Miracle supports 24/5 trading access for eligible instruments and venues. Stock exchange trading hours still follow local market schedules.

### Q5: Is Miracle beginner-friendly?
Yes. Miracle is designed to be simple and transparent, with education, guided onboarding, and clear explanations of costs and market access.
